FAQ

Everything you need to know.

What does 450Digital do?

450Digital builds AI-powered revenue tools for B2B teams. The flagship product, Recon, gives reps instant account intelligence — financials, hiring signals, competitive context, and personalized outreach — in under 30 seconds. The Agent Suite covers SMS and voice agents that reactivate dormant pipeline, recover abandoned carts, answer after-hours inquiries, and protect your reputation. Foundational demand generation services and training tie it all together.

What is Recon and how does it work?

Recon is an AI account research tool that works as a Chrome extension and integrates natively with Salesforce. When a rep opens any prospect account, Recon automatically pulls SEC filings, recent news, hiring trends, and competitive signals — then surfaces the insights most relevant to your product, along with a personalized outreach email and multi-step cadence. The full brief is ready in under 30 seconds.

How much does personalized outreach improve reply rates?

Personalized outreach based on real account intelligence consistently achieves 2–3× higher reply rates than generic templated messaging. The difference is relevance — when a rep references a buyer's actual strategic priorities, recent announcements, or hiring signals, the message stands out. Recon automates the research required to make every message that specific.

Do the AI agents replace my sales team?

No — they handle the work your team would "never get around to doing": reviving old databases, answering at 2 AM, chasing abandoned carts, replying to reviews. Every agent hands warm, qualified conversations to humans at the right moment, so reps spend their time closing rather than chasing.

What if our funnel fundamentals aren't in place yet?

That's exactly what the Foundations services are for. Assessments, messaging work, nurture design, and attribution audits get the strategy right so AI has something solid to amplify — and the Demand Generation Foundations training brings your team along.
