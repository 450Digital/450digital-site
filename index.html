450Digital builds AI-powered revenue tools for B2B sales teams. Our flagship product, Scout, gives reps instant account intelligence — financials, hiring signals, competitive context, and personalized outreach — in under 30 seconds. We also offer SMS and voice agents that reactivate dormant pipeline and handle inbound lead qualification around the clock.

Scout is an AI account research tool that works as a Chrome extension and integrates natively with Salesforce. When a rep opens any prospect account, Scout automatically pulls SEC filings, recent news, hiring trends, and competitive signals — then surfaces the insights most relevant to your product. It also generates a personalized outreach email and multi-step cadence tailored to that account's current priorities. The full brief is ready in under 30 seconds.

Personalized outreach based on real account intelligence consistently achieves 2–3× higher reply rates than generic templated messaging. The difference comes from relevance — when a rep references a buyer's actual strategic priorities, recent announcements, or hiring signals, the message stands out. Scout automates the research required to make every outreach feel bespoke, at scale.

Reps who manually research accounts typically spend 45–60 minutes per prospect gathering context from LinkedIn, news, SEC filings, and company websites. Scout compresses that to under 30 seconds. Teams using Scout report an average 60% reduction in pre-call research time — time that goes back into selling, following up, and closing.

Yes. Scout is built to work inside Salesforce — reps can access full account briefs without leaving their CRM. It also runs as a standalone Chrome extension for use on LinkedIn, company websites, or anywhere reps are doing research. No tab switching, no copy-pasting between tools.

Scout pulls from SEC EDGAR filings (10-K, 10-Q, 8-K), real-time news and press releases, public hiring data and job postings, and company web presence. It then applies AI to surface the signals most relevant to your product — strategic priorities, risk factors, capability gaps, and expansion signals — rather than just dumping raw data.

The SMS agents handle database reactivation (re-engaging dormant leads at scale), abandoned cart recovery, and speed-to-lead follow-up for new inbound inquiries. The voice agents manage inbound calls — qualifying leads, answering common questions, and booking meetings — so your team focuses on high-value conversations. Both run 24/7 without adding headcount.
